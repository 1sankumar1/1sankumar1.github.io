APIs in P&C Insurance Industry

Nov 12, 2015

Insurance industry is seeing increasing usage of public APIs. The current interactions between insurance organizations, partners, distributors, regulatory entities etc. provide innovative ways to use APIs both in private and public space. Additionally, technology advances (IoT, vehile telematics etc) are making organizations adopt API centric integration strategies.

Currrently, not many in industry (insurers and others) have publicly exposed APIs. However, this will change in coming years as value in API-fying the assets becomes more visible.

NOTE: I am not actively working on this list anymore.
